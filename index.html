I'm Laavanya, and I am broadly interested in mechanisms of gene regulation, genetic variants in populations and biology education

I am currently PhD candidate at Duke University in the Genetics and Genomics Program.

For my thesis, I am investigating at the role of regulatory regions and non-coding variants in the context of the reproductive disorder - polycystic ovary syndrome with Dr. Tim Reddy in the Duke Center for Advanced Genomic Technologies. I use both computational and experimental approaches to address these questions. I am also a Duke Preparing Future Faculty Fellow and Teaching on Purpose Fellow.
