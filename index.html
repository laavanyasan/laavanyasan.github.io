I'm Laavanya and I am a geneticist broadly interested in mechanisms of gene regulation, genetic variants in populations and biology education

Currently, I am a PhD candidate in Duke University in the Genetics and Genomics Program.

For my thesis, I am investigating at the role of regulatory regions and non-coding variants in the context of the reproductive disorder - polycystic ovary syndrome with Dr. Tim Reddy in the Duke Center for Advanced Genomic Technologies. I use both computational and experimental approaches to address these questions.

I am also a Preparing Future Faculty Fellow and Teaching on Purpose Fellow.
