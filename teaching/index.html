As an educator, I aim to use biology and genetics as a way for student to explore their ideas about the natural world and understanding the interconnected nature of life. I also emphasize using real-world examples and historical contexts as themes with which students can engage with the themes and reflect on the current narratives of science and biology.

Throughout graduate school, teaching has been one of my focal points through academic coursework, my peadagogical training certificate of college teaching, my work as a Teaching on Purpose Fellow and educational outreach activities
